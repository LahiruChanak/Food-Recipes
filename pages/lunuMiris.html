Sri Lankan Lunu Miris

Difficulty
Beginner

Prep Time      Cook Time      Total Time
10 mins       -           10 mins

Servings
4

Description

Sri Lankan Lunu Miris is a vibrant and bold condiment that packs a punch of flavors, combining spicy, citrusy, salty, and umami notes. It's incredibly easy to make in under 10 minutes with no cooking required. This recipe is suitable for the 4 servings.

Ingredients












Instructions

  1. Add chili flakes, Maldive fish chips, salt, and Chili powder into the mortar and grind them until there aren't any big pieces of Maldive fish chips left. They could be hard to chew.



  2. Add the onions. Pound with the pestle until onions and chilies are thoroughly mixed and turned into a rough paste.



  3. Add lime juice. Mix well with a spoon. Taste and adjust the salt and lime if needed. Lunu Miris should have a prominent citrus flavor. It's best to serve it freshly made.



Enjoy your meal !
